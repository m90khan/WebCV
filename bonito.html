Bonito Furnitures

Custom made furniture to your need.

Bonito Furniture provides custom made furniture according to customer needs. The company needed to tell their story. When it comes to furniture, it is not just the furniture, it is the unique experience and feels that creates a difference. The original website heavily focused on images. I redesigned the overall website and minimize the repetitions and converted the design into a WordPress theme.

Analysis

The original site was heavy on repetitions of content with over 15 pages, lacking clarity and creating Confusion.

Approach

The first launch of the website needed to be straight to the point, communicating the rebrand in a way that is easy for the visitor to understand and navigate but it is also needed to bear in mind that the site may grow in the future. The main focus was first on giving a clear message. And doing so in a way that would set them apart from the rest of their competitors.
